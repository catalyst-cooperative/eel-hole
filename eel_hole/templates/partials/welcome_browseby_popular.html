Want to explore the most widely used PUDL tables?

  • out_eia__yearly_generators and out_eia__monthly_generators - Time series of all generator attributes including calculated capacity factor, heat rate, fuel cost per MMBTU and fuel cost per MWh.
  • core_eia860__scd_plants - Slowly changing dimension (SCD) table describing attributes of plants which may vary from year to year. Compiled from across all EIA-860 and EIA-923 data.
  • core_epacems__hourly_emissions - Hourly time series of emissions and plant operational data as required by 40 CFR Part 75.
  • core_nrelatb__yearly_projected_cost_performance - Annual time series of projections of costs and performance for various technologies.
  • out_vcerare__hourly_available_capacity_factor - Hourly time series of estimated county-averaged capacity factors for wind and solar generating facilities across the contiguous United States (US), to be used as a tool and input for resource adequacy modeling and planning.
  • core_eiaaeo__yearly_projected_fuel_cost_in_electric_sector_by_type - Annual time series of projected fuel prices for the electric power sector, including electricity-only and combined-heat-and-power plants that have a regulatory status.
  • out_eia__yearly_utilities - Annual time series of all EIA utility attributes.
  • core_eia860__scd_generators_energy_storage - Slowly changing dimension (SCD) table describing attributes of energy storage which may vary from year to year.
  • core_eia930__hourly_interchange - Hourly time series of balancing authority interchange.
  • out_eia923__monthly_fuel_receipts_costs - Monthly time series of fuel deliveries to power plants.
  • out_ferc714__hourly_planning_area_demand - Hourly time series of electricity demand by planning area.
  • core_nrelatb__yearly_projected_financial_cases_by_scenario - Annual time series of additional financial assumptions for NREL ATB projections that also vary by technology innovation scenario (scenario_atb), tax credit case (model_tax_credit_case_nrelatb), and cost recovery period (cost_recovery_period_years).
  • out_eia__yearly_plants - Annual time series of all EIA plant attributes.
